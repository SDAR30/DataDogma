Technological Mirage. The pursuit of a "paradise" on Earth, or a utopian existence, has been a part of human aspirations for millennia. 
- transferring consciousness into a system (San Junipero)
- VR will allow us to immerse ourselves in idealized, customizable worlds where we can escape the harsh realities of the physical world. (Ready Player One)
- Transhumanism: This movement believes in using technology to enhance the human body, mind, and lifespan, with the ultimate aim of merging humans with machines or even achieving immortality. (Altered carbon, Ghost in the shell)
- Post-Scarcity Economy: The idea that technological advancements might one day eliminate the need for work. (Star Trek, WALL-E: all their needs are met by machines)
- Space Colony. 
- Technological Singularity:  a form of paradise through boundless technological capabilities.
